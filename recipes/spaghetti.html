Spaghetti

Description

This recipe is a slightly Cuban take on traditional spaghetti from the expertise and genius of my mother.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti rigati
  • tomato basil sauce (in the jar)
  • ground beef
  • sazón
  • heavy cream
  • garlic powder
  • white cooking wine
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Place your ground beef in a large pan and turn heat on High.
  2. Begin to stir/sautee the ground beef while spreading the beef apart evenly throughout the pan. Add your sazón.
  3. Once the beef is browned and fully cooked, add your garlic powder.
  4. Add your tomato basil sauce to the ground beef. Stir.
  5. Add a dash of heavy cream in an 'S' shape in your ground beef/sauce pan. Stir.
  6. In a deep pot, bring water to a boil.
  7. Once the water has reached boiling, add your spaghetti rigate. Keep spaghetti noodles separated as they boil to avoid sticking.
  8. Once your spaghetti noodles are properly cooked, strain the water out of the pot.
  9. Transfer noodles back into the pot and add olive oil to the noodles to prevent sticking.
  10. Serve and enjoy.
