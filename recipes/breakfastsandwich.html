Breakfast Sandwich

Description

This is an expertly-made breakfast sandwich. Very few know how to construct a sandwich such as this one. This sandwich includes melty cheese
and ham with every bit you take. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • bread of your choice (sourdough, rye, or artesano recommended)
  • ham
  • swiss cheese
  • 2-3 eggs(brown)

Steps

  1. Toast your bread in a toaster. Be sure to make it nice and burnt, this recipe does not like soft, white, untoasted bread. It calls for dark bread, near-burnt
    if you can stand it.
  2. While your bread is toasting, crack your eggs and whisk them up for the scrambled eggs. This step can be altered to your own egg preferences, if fried
    eggs are more your speed.
  3. Scramble or fry your eggs. Your bread should be toasted by now, so the next step is to place the swiss cheese and ham onto your slices of toast. Make
    sure that the cheese is sitting on the bread rather than the ham, as this is how we achieve nice, melty cheese.
  4. With your slices of toast now decorated with swiss cheese and ham on each respective slice, place them in a toaster oven to melt the cheese and warm up
    the ham. We do not accept cold, slimey ham.
  5. Once the eggs are scrambled and the cheese is melted to perfection, it is time to take your toast out of the toaster oven and place the scrambled eggs on
    one of the slices. Top the other slice overtop.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
